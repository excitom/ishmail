Show Options

The five remaining options in the Composition window pull-down menu are the show options. They enable you to control whether certain Header Fields fields are displayed in the window. To cause a field to be displayed in the Composition window, click the option. To cause a field to be removed from the display, click the option again. The options are:

Show CC:
Click this option to display the carbon copy field.

Show Bcc:
Click this option to display the blind carbon copy field.

Show Fcc:
Click this option to display the forward carbon copy field.

Show Additional:
Click this option to display the other headers field.

Show Settings Panel
Click this option to display the Add signature, Wrap text, Check addresses, and Message type: panels at the bottom of the Composition window.
