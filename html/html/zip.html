Uncompress Command

The Ishmail files are compressed, to reduce the time needed for download. There are two pairs of commands, commonly found on Unix systems, for compressing and uncompressing data: compress/uncompress and gzip/gunzip.

compress/uncompress are the standard commands. gzip/gunzip do a better job of compression, thus creating smaller files, but they are not installed on all systems.

Choose gunzip if it's available on your system, otherwise choose uncompress.
